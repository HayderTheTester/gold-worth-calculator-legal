Privacy Policy

Gold Worth Calculator — by Mithqal

Effective: March 1, 2026

🛠 Overview

Gold Worth Calculator (“the App”) is a utility application that helps you calculate the value of gold items using live market prices and real-time currency exchange rates.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights. We are committed to handling your information transparently and responsibly.

Short version: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Gold items you save stay on your device only. Advertising is provided by Google AdMob, which may collect data as described below.

📁 Information We Collect

Information collected directly by the App

  • Gold item data — weight, unit, and purity values you enter into the calculator. This data is saved locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences / iOS NSUserDefaults. It is never uploaded to any server.
  • Selected currency — your preferred display currency is stored locally on your device.

The App does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information.

🔔 Advertising — Google AdMob

The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve relevant ads, AdMob may collect and process the following data:

  • Device advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • Coarse location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • Device model, OS version, and language settings
  • App usage data (e.g., which ad was shown and whether it was clicked)
  • IP address

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policies. We do not receive, store, or have access to any of this advertising data.

For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy and How Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps .

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → “Delete advertising ID” or “Opt out of Ads Personalization”
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable “Allow Apps to Request to Track”

🌐 Third-Party Services

The App fetches live data from the following external APIs. Each request is made over HTTPS and may include your device’s IP address as part of the normal network connection. No personal data is intentionally sent.

gold-api.com Live gold spot price (XAU/USD)
open.er-api.com Real-time currency exchange rates
Google AdMob In-app banner advertising

These services operate under their own terms of service and privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. We are not responsible for the data practices of these third parties.

🔒 Data Storage & Security

All data saved by the App (your gold items and currency preference) is stored exclusively on your local device using the operating system’s standard key-value storage. This data:

  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Is not backed up to cloud services by the App itself (device-level backups, if enabled by you, may include it)
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App

We do not operate any backend servers, databases, or analytics services that receive data from the App.

👤 Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can address the situation.

✅ Your Rights

Because the App does not collect personal data, there is generally nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our end. For data collected by Google AdMob, you may exercise your rights directly with Google via their Data & Privacy dashboard .

If you reside in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California (CCPA), you have additional rights regarding your personal data. Please contact the respective third-party service providers directly, as they are the data controllers for advertising-related data.

📋 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. When we make changes, we will update the “Effective” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
